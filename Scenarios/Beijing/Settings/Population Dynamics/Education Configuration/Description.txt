Education School.csv 
    It defines the educational requirements for students at different education levels. It includes the following columns:
    Education: The education level, represented by a numerical identifier (note: Education levels should start from 0, representing preschool age children.).
    School: The type of school (represented by a numerical identifier) where students at this education level are expected to study.
    Duration: The number of years that students need to spend at the corresponding school for their education level.

Education Progression.csv 
    This table defines the probability of students advancing to the next education level after completing their current level of education.

Education Progression Details.csv
    This table defines the probability of a student transitioning from their current education level (Education1) to the next education level (Education2).
